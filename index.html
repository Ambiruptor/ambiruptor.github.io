Ambiruptor

The Ambiguation Interruptor

Introduction

Welcome on the website of the Ambiruptor project. The disambiguation problem consist of finding the correct sense of an ambiguous word in a given sentence. Our target is to use learning models in order to be able to guess the correct meanings of words with the highest possible accuracy.
